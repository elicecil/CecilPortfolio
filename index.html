Eli Cecil is an illustrator and designer in Grand Rapids, MI. After completing that whole K-12 thing, Eli earned a BFA in graphic design from Western Kentucky University. She now lives with her husband, pitbull, and two cats in Michigan, where she draws cartoons and crafts fun things.

Eli is currently working on her first webcomic, Mementos, planned to launch in 2018.
